Dr. Gaofeng He (中文介绍在这里)

I am an Associate Professor at the School of Internet of Things, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. My research interests include network traffic analysis and obfuscation, digital twin for IoT security, data security, and the application of AI in security protection and assessment.

My publication lists are here: Papers in English and Papers in Chinese. The main ideas and contributions of our papers will also be presented in the Research Progress.

My email address is hegaofeng at njupt dot edu dot cn. I am always happy to discuss interesting research topics with others.
